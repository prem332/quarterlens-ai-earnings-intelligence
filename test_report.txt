## Executive Summary
- Apple reported nine‑month net sales of $313,695 million, up 6% year-to-date [FILING].  
- The filing attributes growth in several regions to higher net sales of iPhone (driven by higher net sales of Pro models) and Services during the third quarter and first nine months of 2025 [FILING].  
- Products gross margin percentage for the third quarter was 46.5%, up from 46.3% a year earlier; products gross margin increased during the third quarter of 2025 compared to the third quarter of 2024 due primarily to a different mix of products and favorable costs, partially offset by tariffs [FILING].  
- [UNVERIFIED] Apple reported total net sales of $94,036 million for FY2025 Q3, up 10% year-over-year.  
- [UNVERIFIED] iPhone net sales rose 13% to $44,582 million and Services rose 13% to $27,423 million for the quarter.  
- [UNVERIFIED] Mac sales increased 15% to $8,046 million; iPad and Wearables, Home and Accessories declined (‑8% and ‑9%, respectively) for the quarter.  
- [UNVERIFIED] Total gross margin improved to $43,718 million (46.5% of sales) versus $39,678 million (46.3%) a year earlier; product gross margin percentage declined modestly while services margin expanded.
